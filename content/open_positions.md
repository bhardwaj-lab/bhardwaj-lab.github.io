# Open positions

## Postdoc positions

Currently, we do not have fully-funded postdoc position available.

We do have possibility to support international candidates for **externally funded** positions, such as [Marie Curie](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships), [EMBO](https://www.embo.org/funding/fellowships-grants-and-career-support/postdoctoral-fellowships/), or [HFSP](https://www.hfsp.org/funding/hfsp-funding/postdoctoral-fellowships) fellowships. We encourage applicants with a **(co)-first author publication** in a relevant field, to contact us **1-year in advance** if they are interested in applying for such funding with our support.

## PhD Position

We currently do not have an open PhD position available.

We are always excited to hear from exceptional candidates. If you are looking for a PhD at the intersection of genomics and computational biology, feel free to [reach out to Vivek](mailto:v.bhardwaj@uu.nl) 6-12 months in advance, so we can consider you for any upcoming opportunity.

## Internships

For students enrolled in a EU/Dutch university master's program, we have possible spots for an internship/thesis project available every 4-6 months in our lab. Please look at our [internships page](https://vblab.org/internships/) for more information.
